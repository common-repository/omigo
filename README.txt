OMIGO delivers a SERVICE that processes financial contributions when you have an account with OMIGO.

== Description ==

EASY-TO-USE WORDPRESS PLUGIN
Process tax deductible donations and give tax receipts automatically!

Major features in OMIGO include:

* Fundraising goal bar widget. Allow your supporters to see your monthly financial goals and progress with our east to use drag-and-drop widget.
* Automated PDF tax contribution e-mails. OMIGO automatically sends a personalized contribution letters with every on-line donation processed through our plug-in.
* Donor database. Keep easy track of your donors and their contributions all in one location.
* Accept recurring donations.

Please note that this plugin will be using/calling our service on the following URL: https://portal.omigo.org

PS: You'll need to register and get your activation token in order to process donations.

REQUIREMENTS TO USE THIS PLUGIN

* You must be a United States of America legal citizen. (You may be asked to submit proof of citizenship prior to registration approval).
* You must only use a U.S. bank account. (No foreign banks permitted).
* You must have a website domain that has a valid SSL certifcate.

== Installation ==

Upload the OMIGO plugin to your website, Activate it, Then register to start processing donations.

1, 2, 3: You're done!

== How to use the donation button: ==

1. Make a custom menu item and then type "#donate" in the url. (See screenshot #1)
It's really that simple! You can use "#donate" for pre-made buttons and custom urls.

2. To show your goal bar, just drag the widget. (See screenshot #2)
We really made this simple!

3. Get notified immediately when a donation is recieved by entering your email. (See screenshot #3)

4. You have two ways for people to donate. 
Use our popup payment window, or our Goal Bar widget.
